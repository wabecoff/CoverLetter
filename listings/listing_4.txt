Department of Medicine Division of Oncology is seeking a full-time Life Science Technician to join our laboratory and assist the research team on translational research projects. In our lab we investigate mechanisms of cancer and liver cancer progression through studies of cultured cells, use of transgenic mouse models and also human specimens. Under the supervision of the PI, this technician will conduct experiments to support ongoing projects in the lab. The position includes work with mice and cultured mammalian cells.
