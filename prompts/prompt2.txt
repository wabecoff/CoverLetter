Use the resume to draft the second paragraph of a cover letter.  The second paragraph should be a brief overview of the applicants background as is relevant to the position. Here, you should include key achievements, skills and specialties that make the applicant particularly suited to perform well in the position. Focus on one or two and provide specific details about the applicants success including measurable impacts the applicant made made.  Keep this paragraph extremely brief. Feel free to use the following format:

As the [previous role] at [previous employer], I was a [previous role description] and was responsible for [responsibilities]. Before that, I worked in [previous role] for [previous employer]. At [previous employer], I developed [previous project], [previous project description], which led to [benefit of previous product].
